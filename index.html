Mission Statement

Our program, Pick Up PC will include regular cleanup events, identifying sources of refuse, proposing mitigation options, defining the impact on our ecosystems, and identifying the benefits of keeping our community cleaner and greener. Our message, together we can make a difference.

Our Goals

  1. Establish collaborative relationships with Park City Municipal, Summit County, The Chamber of Commerce, Recycle Utah, and other educational institutions.
  2. Promote pride and respect in our community.
  3. Produce a documentary film on the blight associated with trash in our environment.
  4. Identify mitigation options
  5. Create a “blueprint” for others to follow based on best practices gleaned from our efforts.
  6. Use our social media platforms to promote both awareness and the beneficial impact of this program.
  7. Establish PCHS as a “beckon” of environmental stewardship.
